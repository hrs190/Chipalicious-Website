Our Story

When Chipalicious launched in 2017, we had one mission: to elevate the humble chip into something extraordinary. Located in Adelaide, we've quickly become a fixture at sporting events, festivals, and other gatherings across South Australia.

Our secret? It's quite simple - our unwavering committment to quality and our love for the community. Our food and friendly faces are a reflection of these values, and we take pride in communicating our values to South Australians through the food that we serve.
